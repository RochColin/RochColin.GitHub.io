Since childhood, I have always loved the arts. I must admit growing up, I had always thought that art was more of a hobby. My jobs after graduating from high school had nothing to do with the arts and it was this absence that made me realize that I must follow my passion and pursue a craft in multimedia.

As a young boy I was, as most boys are, crazy about comics and video. This made me appreciate the genre and aroused my interest in this field. I then realized that I wanted to learn everything about multimedia communication, including, visual communication, different design techniques, animation to name a few.

I am a passionate and dedicated man. When I set a goal, I work hard until I have attained it. My greatest quality is that I am a perfectionist, I will work a project until I am completely satisfied. Whether it is a logo, an art project or a video, I take the time to inquire about the required mediums needed, to make sure that I am well informed and up to date with the latest technologies and techniques. Then I take the time to experiment and explore the possibilities. I am always ready to familiarize myself with the various disciplines required. I must confess that I chase the "high" of a job well done. I have the desire to explore all possible areas included in multi-media as we never know what opportunities these will lead to in life.

I realize that there are several programs offered at different institutions, but as a proud Francophone I wanted to continue my studies in French. It was also important to follow in the footsteps of family members who also completed their studies at USB. I feel privileged to be a student at the Université de St. Boniface. As I approach the end of the multimedia communication program, I intend to pursue a career in animation, whether it be through video, games or comics.
